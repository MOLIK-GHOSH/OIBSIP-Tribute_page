Aman Gupta

born 4 March 1982

Biography

Gupta started his career as an assistant manager with Citi.
He later joined KPMG as a Senior Management Consultant.
Later he co-founded Advanced Telemedia Private Limited and
was elevated to the position of chief executive officer (CEO).
He founded the electronics brand boAt in 2016 with Sameer Mehta.
This company sells headphones, smart watches and speakers.
